Moo is rogue-like shooter game. It aims for stunning visual effects, while keeping art style simple. It offers dynamic and spectacular gameplay.

You are a human kidnapped by evil cows-invaders. You need to fight a way out of their space station. On your way you will be challenged by endless herd of cows and their leaders.
‍
You are equipped with powerful energy weapon. Use it wisely. The weapon has multiple modes that adapts to your style. If you prefer close range combat use sword or shotgun mode. If you want to take them down one by one in precise manner use pistol. If you aim for maximum destruction machine gun and grenade launcher modes are for you. On your way collect upgrades from dead cow-generals bodies to customize your build and increase your strength.
